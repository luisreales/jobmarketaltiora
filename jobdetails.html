Acerca del empleo

About Us

We're kommit, a tech staffing company with over 16 years of experience connecting Latin American talent with US-based companies. We are currently supporting a client in the real estate sector to scale their high-performance ads engine.


About the Role

We are seeking a Senior Ad-Tech Engineer to join a specialized team focused on programmatic advertising. You will be responsible for the ad-serving pipeline, from bid request handling through creative delivery, ensuring the platform operates efficiently in low-latency environments.


In this role, you will work with high-scale AWS architectures to solve complex problems related to real-time bidding (RTB) and auction mechanics. This is an ideal opportunity for a "language-agnostic" engineer who prioritizes deep industry knowledge of Ad-Tech protocols over a specific coding language and wants a significant voice in technical and architectural decisions.


What You'll Be Doing
  • Design and operate scalable systems on AWS that handle variable, auction-driven traffic patterns using auto-scaling groups and managed Kafka or Kinesis.
  • Manage the RTB lifecycle, including bid request/response mechanics, auction logic, and win notification handling.
  • Integrate and build against ad server platforms, with SpringServe experience being a strong differentiator.
  • Ensure compliance with IAB technical standards such as VAST/VPAID, ads.txt, and sellers.json.
  • Develop data pipelines for reporting, attribution, frequency capping, and event stream processing.
  • Optimize low-latency lookups and logging using DynamoDB, Redis, S3, and Athena or Redshift.


Required Qualifications
  • 10+ years of professional experience in software engineering.
  • Direct experience with programmatic advertising systems and the OpenRTB specification.
  • Hands-on expertise in designing and operating high-throughput AWS infrastructure (EC2, ECS, EKS).
  • Deep understanding of IAB technical standards and the ad-serving pipeline.
  • English C1 preferred (minimum B2) for daily collaboration with U.S.-based teams.
  • Must be based in Latin America.


Preferred Qualifications
  • Strong experience with SpringServe AdServer integrations.
  • Header bidding experience, including Prebid.js or server-side implementations.
  • Familiarity with privacy and consent frameworks (TCF, GPP, CCPA signal handling).
  • Exposure to CTV/OTT ad delivery.
  • Background working at SSPs, DSPs, or ad networks.


Compensation & Work Arrangement
  • Monthly compensation: $5,000 USD.
  • Contract type: Independent Contractor.
  • Work model: 100% Remote - LATAM.
  • Availability: Full-time, 40 hours per week.


What We Offer
  • Professional growth opportunities
  • A collaborative, supportive team
